About Pineapple Ripeness Detection

Our Pineapple Ripeness Detection System is an innovative solution designed to help farmers and suppliers determine the optimal ripeness of Mauritius pineapples with accuracy and efficiency. By using advanced image processing and machine learning techniques, our system improves harvest timing and reduces post-harvest losses, leading to better-quality produce and increased profitability.

Benefits of Using Our System

Accurate Harvest Timing: The system helps farmers pick pineapples at their peak ripeness, ensuring optimal flavor and quality.

Reduced Post-Harvest Losses: By accurately determining ripeness, farmers can reduce losses caused by under- or overripe fruits.

Increased Productivity: Automated detection saves time and effort, enabling farmers to focus on other important aspects of farming.
